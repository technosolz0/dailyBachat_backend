Account Deletion

We are sorry to see you go. Following Google Play policy, we provide a direct way to delete your account and all associated data.

Steps to delete from App:

  1. Open DailyBachat app
  2. Go to Profile screen
  3. Tap on Delete Account
  4. Confirm Delete Permanently

Alternatively, you can contact our support team to request immediate deletion:

Email Support

Note: Deletion will permanently remove all your transactions, invoices, and profile data from our servers. This action cannot be undone.
